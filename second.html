KFC Edible Nail Polish

Fried chicken flavoured nail polish - hell yeah!! Recently launched in Hong Kong, KFC's nail polish range brings together two of our favourite things ever - fried chicken and nail art. The promotional video above is a creative project by Film and Animation Director Helen Clemens. The range only has two colours at the moment but these are far from disappointing - "Hot & Spicy" is a fabulous deep orange, perfect for summer, whilst "Original Recipe" is a nude shade with black flecks. This trend is finger lickin' good for real!

VR Nails

Taking the beauty world by storm, the VR nails trend is the hottest thing in nail art right now! Imagine being able to preview and customise your nail design by way of an extensive and infinite database of nail design combinations before you buy - well now you can!

Those based in the UK can enjoy this exciting advance in nail art by popping into WAH Nails, Soho, London. WAH's salon of the future allows customers to create their nail design by way of VR; using a Samsung Gear headset with Leap Motion technology you can play around with WAH's extensive database to select and customise your design - and the options are endless. Once you've created your design you can either print off your designs as press-on nails, order your customised nail colour to use at home or get an in-house nail artist to bring your vision to life in salon!
